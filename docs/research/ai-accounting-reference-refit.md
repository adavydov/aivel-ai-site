# Aivel 2.0 refit: AI accounting reference patterns

Scope: third-party references are used as layout and communication patterns only. Copy, branding, screenshots and assets are original Aivel materials or neutral mock data.

## Target communication chain

1. Product moment first: a leader receives a concrete signal.
2. Problem recognition: events the owner usually learns too late.
3. Mechanism: sources → reliable accounting → company knowledge → business context → Aivel signal.
4. Trust: audit trail, human exceptions, controlled roles and source-linked evidence.
5. Routes: owner/CEO, business accounting, large-company process automation, accounting-company partner.

## Implementation notes

- Keep the current Aivel 2.0 shell: white background, left rail, black and blue palette, large editorial typography.
- Add stronger surfaces only where they carry communication value: signal card, proof strip, process card, workflow surface.
- Avoid dashboard-heavy visuals. The core product promise is an invisible product: the important message arrives to the decision-maker.
- Use future-state product features only as target-product language, not as measured claims: rules of importance, normal-business comparison, internal-decision brief.
